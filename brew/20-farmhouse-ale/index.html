#20 Swedish-hopped farmhouse ale

Brew date: 2020-11-30

Brewer: alexis

Malt budget

  • 1000 g wheat
  • 1500 g light munich
  • 2000 g pilsner

Strike water

20 L at 75 deg, with some juniper branches

Infused in a bucket with the grain, 68 deg. 11:40. Kept under wool blanket

Warming 10 L sparge water

Hops to chose from:

  • SWE26 Frebo 3.9 %AA <-
  • SWE51 Klev 6.1 %AA <-
  • SWE52 Brusbo 2.8 %AA
  • SWE08 Spångby
  • SWE?3 Grimsarbo?
  • SWE13 Svalöf M 1.5 %AA

12:40 end of mash-in. Started to lauter. Vorlauf a bit. Strike water is hot :/ (80+ deg)

BATCH 1 KLEV

Boiling 11.5 litres of wort

Hop schedule

  • 20g 6.1%AA, 60 minutes, 34 IBU 13:50
  • 30g at whirlpool, couple of minutes

FIlling bucket at 14:50. 7.5 litres collected

Fermentation

Pitched with Hisingen farmhouse yeast at around 21 degrees. After 24 hours, nothing happened, pitched with MJ Saison

BATCH 2 FREBO

Boiling 11 litres of wort

Hop schedule

  • 30g 4%AA 60 minutes, 33 IBU, 15:40
  • 20g whirlpool

Fermentation

Pitched with Voss

ORIGINAL GRAVITY: 1.060 Cooling outside. Pretty cold temperature.

Bottling 2020-10-29

KLEV/Saison: FG:1.007

FREBO/Voss: FG: 1.010

Primed with 50g each batch. Aiming at 2.5 volumes CO2.
