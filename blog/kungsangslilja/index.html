2021-05-21  Kungsängslilja

A beautiful kungsangslilja (Fritillaria meleagris L.) in the forest next door

A pretty picture today, with one of my favourite flower: kungsängslilja in Swedish (and a great name in French: Fritillaire pintade). It is the official flower of Uppland province, which encompasses Uppsala.
